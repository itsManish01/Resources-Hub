About Us

This website was built during Weekend of Code 2022 under the event DevJam 5.0 in guidance with our senior mentors and owned by first year students. The primary objective is to reach the excited First Year students to give them a one-stop solution to all their doubts, ranging from academic to personal doubts during their college life. The website holds sections for clearing their academic doubts regarding notes of each subject in First Year, as well as, regarding doubts of starting their much-awaited journey of Coding in the form of Competitive Programming to give them a kickstart to their dream packages. We also hold a section of a virtual campus tour, where freshers can seek to the pictures of the various components of our college.
